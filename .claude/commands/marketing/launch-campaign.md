# Launch Marketing Campaign

I need you to plan and execute a comprehensive marketing campaign across multiple channels.

## Context

**Campaign Goal**: $ARGUMENTS

(Examples: "Product launch for new AI feature", "Drive 10k signups in 30 days", "Rebrand announcement", "Black Friday promotion")

## Your Task

Route this to the **content-creator** agent who will orchestrate with platform-specific agents (tiktok-strategist, instagram-curator, twitter-engager, reddit-community-builder):

1. **Campaign Strategy**:
   - Define target audience (demographics, psychographics, behaviors)
   - Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
   - Choose channels (TikTok, Instagram, Twitter, Reddit, email, paid ads)
   - Create messaging framework (value prop, key messages, CTAs)
   - Budget allocation across channels

2. **Content Planning**:
   - Content calendar (4-6 weeks)
   - Platform-specific content formats:
     - TikTok: 15-60s videos, trending sounds, challenges
     - Instagram: Reels, Stories, carousel posts
     - Twitter: Threads, polls, quote tweets
     - Reddit: AMAs, value-first posts, community engagement
   - Repurposing strategy (1 video → 10+ pieces of content)

3. **Channel-Specific Execution**:
   **TikTok** (tiktok-strategist):
   - Trending audio/hashtag research
   - Hook-first video scripts
   - Posting schedule (3-5x per day)

   **Instagram** (instagram-curator):
   - Visual aesthetic consistency
   - Reels + Stories + Feed strategy
   - Engagement tactics (polls, questions, quizzes)

   **Twitter** (twitter-engager):
   - Thread templates
   - Real-time engagement strategy
   - Community building tactics

   **Reddit** (reddit-community-builder):
   - Subreddit targeting
   - Value-first content (90% value, 10% promotion)
   - AMA planning if applicable

4. **Paid Advertising** (if budget available):
   - Platform selection (Meta Ads, TikTok Ads, Google Ads)
   - Ad creative variations (3-5 per platform)
   - Targeting parameters
   - Budget pacing (daily/lifetime)
   - A/B testing plan

5. **Influencer/Partnership Strategy**:
   - Identify relevant influencers (micro: 10k-100k, macro: 100k-1M)
   - Outreach templates
   - Collaboration formats (sponsored posts, affiliate, co-creation)
   - Tracking links and UTM parameters

6. **Measurement & Optimization**:
   - KPIs by channel:
     - TikTok: Views, engagement rate (> 5%), follower growth
     - Instagram: Reach, saves, shares, profile visits
     - Twitter: Impressions, engagement rate (> 2%), clicks
     - Reddit: Upvotes, comments, subreddit karma
   - Analytics setup (Google Analytics, UTM tracking)
   - Weekly performance reviews
   - Budget reallocation based on performance

## Deliverables

- Campaign brief (goals, audience, messaging, channels)
- Content calendar (4-6 weeks, all platforms)
- Platform-specific content (scripts, captions, visuals)
- Paid ad creative and targeting setup
- Influencer outreach list and templates
- Analytics dashboard (KPIs, tracking links)
- Weekly performance reports

## Campaign Timeline

**Week 1: Planning & Setup**
- Finalize strategy and content calendar
- Create initial content batch (2 weeks ahead)
- Set up tracking and analytics

**Week 2-5: Execution**
- Daily posting across platforms
- Community engagement (respond to comments, DMs)
- Monitor performance, optimize underperformers
- A/B test ad creative

**Week 6: Analysis & Iteration**
- Comprehensive performance report
- Identify top-performing content
- Recommendations for future campaigns
